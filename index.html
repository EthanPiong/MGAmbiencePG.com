Ambience Residence

@ Pulau Gadong

Ambience Residence @ Pulau Gadong is a residential development located in the beautiful City of Melaka. Positioned in a picturesque area with lush greenery and a serene river as the backdrop, this development offers a tranquil lifestyle. Residents can enjoy stunning views of the surrounding paddy fields and natural landscape.

Designed to provide both affordability and comfort, Ambience Residence is equipped with a range of amenities to enhance the living experience. The property features 24-hour security, ensuring a safe and worry-free environment for residents. Additionally, sheltered parking spaces are available for convenience.
